# Contributing

Thanks for wanting to help. The KB needs to stay accurate to be useful, and a wrong KB is worse than no KB — so PRs are reviewed against a strict sourcing standard.

## Rules of evidence

When changing any ability, mechanic, counter, or tier entry, cite an **official Blizzard source**:

- ✅ **Hero pages** at `overwatch.blizzard.com/en-us/heroes/<hero>/`
- ✅ **Patch notes** at `overwatch.blizzard.com/en-us/news/patch-notes/` (link the specific patch)

### Sources we don't accept

- ❌ **Fandom wiki** (`overwatch.fandom.com`) — frequently months stale on reworks.
- ❌ **Stadium Powers / Stadium gameplay** — Stadium is a separate mode with mid-match ability customization. A Stadium Power is **not** evidence of base 5v5 Role Queue behavior.
- ❌ **6v6 Open Queue** — different role balance, different meta.
- ❌ **YouTube / Reddit / streamer claims** for mechanics. Fine as context for "the community thinks X is strong"; not as evidence for what an ability does.

Third-party stat sites (Overbuff, owherostats, etc.) are OK as supporting signal for **tier placement and pick rates** — not for ability mechanics.

## PR checklist

- [ ] One-line summary of what changed.
- [ ] Source link to the Blizzard page or patch notes that supports it.
- [ ] Patch version it reflects (e.g. "post May 12, 2026 patch").
- [ ] For ability/counter changes, confirmed the behavior holds in **5v5 Role Queue** (not Stadium, not Open Queue).

## Style

- Keep the KB scannable — bullets, short lines, no walls of prose.
- Name the specific ability in counter entries ("Zarya's beam ignores Defense Matrix"), not vague matchup feelings.
- Use exact ability names from the Blizzard hero page (capitalization included).

Be patient on review — this is a hobby project.
